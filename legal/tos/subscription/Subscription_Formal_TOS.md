Serial 1. Subscription Model

QuantumTrader-AI™ operates on a paid monthly subscription basis, granting users access to the platform’s artificial intelligence tools, analytics, simulations, and automated decision-support systems.

The subscription fee provides platform access only and does not constitute trading capital, investment funds, assets under management, or any form of financial stake.


---

Serial 2. Subscription Fee, Duration, and Renewal

Billing Cycle: Monthly

Access Period: Each successful subscription payment grants 30 consecutive days of access from the date of payment.

Renewal Condition: Continued access beyond the initial 30-day period requires payment of a new monthly subscription fee.

Fee Structure: Flat-rate subscription

Indicative Fee: ₦10,000 per month (subject to revision prior to or after public release)


Failure to renew upon expiration results in automatic suspension of access until payment is made.


---

Serial 3. Separation from Trading Capital

Subscription fees are strictly separate from any funds a user may choose to deploy for trading purposes.

Accordingly:

Subscription payments are not used for trading

Subscription payments do not guarantee profits, returns, or performance

Trading capital remains under the sole control and responsibility of the user


QuantumTrader-AI™ does not pool, manage, custody, or deploy user funds as part of the subscription.
